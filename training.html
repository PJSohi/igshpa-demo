Training

Training is vital in any industry, especially in the ground source heat pump industry. Without training and accredited installers, ground source heat pumps have a greater chance of being installed incorrectly. Faulty installations can result in customer dissatisfaction and possible injury. IGSHPA training is the best way to learn accepted ground source heat pump standards and procedures and the best way to get ahead in the industry. filter_3Certified GeoExchange Designer

Owners of commercial, institutional and industrial buildings are concerned about energy costs and the environmental impact of their buildings. Whether they are developing a new facility or upgrading their existing buildings, many building owners are talking to their architects and engineers about the feasibility of installing a GCHP system.

In 2000, IGSHPA and the Association of Energy Engineers (AEE) developed the Certified GeoExchange Designer course.
