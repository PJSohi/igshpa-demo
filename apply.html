paymentMEMBERSHIP PROCESS

Membership and training certification in IGSHPA Canada are independent, although a bundled fee is available for members who become certified or certified individuals who become members.

Please send your membership fees via cheque (including applicable HST/GST) to our Ottawa office.

Please download our application form in pdf, complete & mail to IGSHPA Canada, 7885 Jock Trail, Ottawa, ON K0A 2Z0.

For questions, please email NetZeroPlus@email.com or telephone (343) 882-7900.
